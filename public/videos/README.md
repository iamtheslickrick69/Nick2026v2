# Hero Background Video

Add your hero background video here as `hero-background.mp4`.

## Requirements:
- Format: MP4 (H.264 codec recommended)
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9 or wider
- Resolution: 1920x1080 or higher
- Duration: 10-30 seconds (will loop)
- Size: Optimize for web (< 5MB recommended)

## Recommended Content:
- Professional office or workplace environment
- Subtle motion (not too distracting)
- Warm, inviting atmosphere
- Matches LoopSync brand colors (#E07850, #1B7F8E)

## Fallback:
If no video is added, the section will show the dark overlay with a solid background color.

## How to Add:
1. Export your video as MP4
2. Name it `hero-background.mp4`
3. Place it in this `/public/videos/` directory
4. The video will automatically load on the homepage hero section
